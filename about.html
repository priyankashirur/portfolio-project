My life outside of work

Punching above my weight class

Boxing is more than just a sport; it's a journey of self-discovery and empowerment. Through rigorous training and intense matches, I’ve learned discipline, resilience, and the importance of pushing my limits. The thrill of competition and the camaraderie within the boxing community fuel my passion for the ring.

Shaping yound minds

Tutoring is my way of inspiring the next generation. I work with young learners to nurture their curiosity and build their confidence in various subjects. With a focus on personalized learning and supportive guidance, I aim to help students reach their academic potential and foster a love for knowledge.

Eating my way through Los Angeles

After living in the Bay Area for 15 years, I was spoiled for choices in the culinary world. Los Angeles boasts a diverse and exciting culinary scene, and I love testing it out. My adventures take me through the city’s vibrant food landscape, where I explore everything from trendy food trucks to hidden gems. Each dish tells a story, and I relish the opportunity to discover and share the flavors that make LA a food lover’s paradise.
